Water Footprint Calculator

This is an interactive app that estimates your daily water footprint based on your lifestyle habits. It uses food, consumption, and household activity inputs to calculate approximate water usage.

🔗 Live App

👉 Launch the Streamlit App

🧠 Background

Water footprint is a key indicator of water use. It reflects both direct and indirect water usage. This tool aims to increase awareness of personal consumption habits and their environmental impact.
