💧 Water Footprint Calculator

Estimate your daily water consumption and discover how your habits impact the environment.

🌍 Why Water Footprint?

Water footprint measures the total volume of freshwater used to produce the goods and services we consume. This includes both direct use (like showers or washing clothes) and indirect use (like the water needed to produce your morning coffee or your clothes).

“What gets measured gets managed.” – Peter Drucker

🧠 How It Works

The calculator asks a series of questions about your food choices, daily activities, and consumption patterns. Each response is linked to average water use values based on datasets from Water Footprint Network and academic literature.

The app then computes your estimated daily water usage in liters.

🔗 Try the App

👉 Launch the Streamlit App

🛠️ About the Project

This project was developed as part of a broader initiative to make environmental awareness tools more accessible. It was coded in Python using the Streamlit library and is open source.

Feel free to explore the source code or adapt it to your own needs.
