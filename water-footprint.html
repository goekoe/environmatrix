💧 Water Footprint Calculator

This interactive tool helps estimate the water footprint of various daily activities, such as food consumption, energy use, and household habits.

🔍 What It Does

The calculator allows users to enter their daily habits (e.g., shower duration, meat consumption, etc.) and provides an estimated total water footprint in liters/day or m³/year.

🛠️ How It Works

Built with Python and Streamlit, this app uses default water consumption coefficients based on FAO and peer-reviewed studies. The underlying logic is transparent and easily adjustable.
